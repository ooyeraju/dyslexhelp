About Us

p>This application has been developed for enhancing the overall learning for kids with dyslexia. It has been observed that people with dyslexia are extremely smart but face small issues in recognising words. So, we have developed this application to help them to tackle this, while both listening and reading words. They face problems with words which have multiple syllables. They find it difficult to figure out which letter corresponds to which sound. Using this app, they can practise multiple times and master the words. I have tried to make this application as engaging as possible, also maintaining its simplicity. I really hope that you like this application.
